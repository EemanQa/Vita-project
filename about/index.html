Virtual Information Security Officer (vISO) Services

A Virtual Information Security Officer (vISO) provides expert cybersecurity leadership and hands-on support to help your organization meet compliance requirements and strengthen its overall security posture. Working as an extension of your team, the vISO guides employees, fills capability gaps, and performs the essential security tasks needed to maintain compliance with Commonwealth of Virginia (COV), VITA, and other applicable standards.

How a vISO Supports Your Team

Policy & Procedure Development

  • Develop and maintain security policies aligned with all 18 control families
  • Maintain and update Acceptable Use policies
  • Create clear, actionable procedures for approximately 1,300 security controls
  • Collaborate with internal staff to ensure documentation reflects real-world practices

Control Implementation & Documentation

  • Create implementation artifacts to demonstrate control compliance
  • Organize and maintain evidence for each control, per system
  • Ensure readiness for audits and assessments through proper documentation

Security Planning & Compliance

  • Core Security Documents: SSP, Control Assessment Plans, POA&Ms
  • Configuration & Change Management: Baseline configurations, hardware/software inventories, change documentation
  • Continuity & Incident Response Planning: COOP, DRP, contingency plans, incident response simulations
  • Organization-Wide Planning: Security & Privacy Plans, Comprehensive Information Security Program Plan
  • Risk Management Support: Annual risk assessments, pre-procurement risk evaluations, actionable remediation plans
“With a vISO, you don’t just meet compliance—you build a culture of security.” — vISO Services

Annual and Ongoing Activities

Performed Annually

  • Business Impact Analyses (BIA)
  • Data sensitivity and classification reviews
  • Control revision assessments due to system changes
  • Maintain Interoperability Security Agreements (ISAs) with external partners
  • Coordinate and report on penetration testing

Ongoing Support

  • Vulnerability remediation tracking (30/60/90-day deadlines)
  • Access approvals for onboarding/offboarding
  • Execution of all ISO duties specified by COV and internal controls
  • Security awareness and training programs
  • Phishing campaign management and reporting
  • Occasional & Scheduled Activities: Support for COV RAMP assessments, Cloud Terms & Conditions reviews, quarterly updates in Archer or other GRC platforms

Every Three Years

  • Lead preparation for security audits conducted by VITA or external entities
“Annual and ongoing activities ensure compliance, resilience, and continuous security improvement.” — vISO Services
